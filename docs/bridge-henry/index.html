IMPORTANT NEWS
We regret to announce the decision to dissolve the bridge group
and cease our weekly games at the Henry, effective immediately.

Despite everyone’s best efforts over a long period to grow the pool of regular players to a sustainable 18–20 participants (enough for 3–4 tables of enjoyable competition), we’ve consistently faced the challenge of low attendance. The reality of operating with just 2 to 2.5 tables for many months, coupled with little prospect of improvement, has made it clear that continuing the competition is no longer viable.

We deeply appreciate the support and commitment from the Henry since the beginning.
